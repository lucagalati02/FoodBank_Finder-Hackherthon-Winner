How To Use

Our team has developed a web app that will be simple for all to use! All you must do is fill in the fields requesting a postal/zip code and a desired search radius.

Once you have done this, just click 'Search', then boom! You will be presented with a list of nearby food banks, highlighting which food bank is closest as well as which is highest rated to ensure that you have only the best experience possible. We hope you enjoy using our application!

Mission Statement

Our web app, FoodBank Finder, is designed to help users locate food banks in their area. As a result of Canada's rising economy, food banks had recently had to tend to 1.5 million people in a single month!

We believe that no one should go hungry, and our mission is to make it as easy as possible for people in need to find the resources they need. We have built a comprehensive system to do just that, using various location based technologies to present only the most accurate data.
